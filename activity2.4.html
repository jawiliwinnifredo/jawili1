    • while at first half of a person's life will produce increases in both muscle and both strength.

  • Muscle and bone strength gains, strength training also affects hormone health positively.

  • Strength training is most commonly seen as weight- bearing activity. The exerciser can use body
    • weight or gym equipment to lift. Another way to provide resistance is the ise of bands(Rubber).




There are two terms concerning strength exercises:

  1. A Rep (REPETITION) is one full movement from starting point to finish.

  2. A Set isa a group of repetitions


Upper Body Strength Training


  • Most basic training design is to have anywhere betwwen 6 and 15 reps in a set and performing 3 sets.

  • Some people will go for 6 to 9 reps, some will go to 9 to 12 some will go for 12 to 15 reps in a set.


Lower Body Strength Training


  • The lower body reps must be anywhere between 15 to 25 reps, also with approximate of 3 sets.
